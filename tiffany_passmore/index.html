Tiffany Passmore

Novelist



Tiffany Passmore has been an author for many years. She was born and raised Illinois. While she can live in the fast lane, Tiffany enjoys the quiet with her feline fur babies. She graduated from Rockford College (Undergraduate) and Capella University (Graduate). Tiffany has four novels published and is currently working on sequels. Her epic fantasy The White Lion she considers her crowning achievement and can't wait to release the stunning second book. She also has an online fan fiction titled The Sugar Series that has been ongoing for over a decade.

About Me



I spent a lot of time growing up in libraries and bookstores. I used to read to my younger siblings and my nieces and nephews. One thing I noticed in my favorite novels was a lack of diversity. Oh, there were elves, dwarves, princess and knights, but no POC. I wanted to create a world where my niece could dress up as a princess for Halloween. A world where my nephew could be a knight riding a dragon. I wanted them to see themselves as heroes. Not just the sidekicks, comic relief or thugs portrayed in the media of the time.
